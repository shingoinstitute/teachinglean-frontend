ANNUAL POMS 2017 CONFERENCE

May 5th - 8th, 2017 | Seattle, Washington

OUR NEXT MEETING

A spirited group of researchers, teachers, and practitioners are getting together to start up the College of Operational Excellence (COE) as a sub-group of the Production and Operations Management Society (POMS). This meeting will also serve as a restart for Lean Education Academic Network (LEAN) as we envision the way forward for teaching and research in higher education.

Join us May 4th, 2017 from 1:00 pm - 7:30 pm at this Mini-Conference and Reception that serves as a prelude to the annual POMS Conference.

GET IN ON THE GROUND FLOOR

REGISTER FOR CONFERENCE

If you are interested in getting in on the ground floor and providing input and direction, we would like to see you there. Just register at the POMS website.

The primary objective of the COE is to build a community focused on operational excellence to

  • Foster creation and dissemination of scholarly knowledge.
  • Share teaching and pedagogical material.
  • Exchange information about undergraduate, graduate and other specialized educational programs.
  • Interact with leading practitioners to enrich research and teaching.
