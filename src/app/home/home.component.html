Learning Lean / Lean Learning

We are delighted to invite you to the fourth European Lean Educator Conference (ELEC 2017), 8-9 November 2017 at HAN University of Applied Sciences, Nijmegen, the Netherlands.

The theme of the conference is Learning Lean / Lean Learning, based on the perspective that the main focus of the lean educator should be on the learning process of his/her customer (student, employee, team or organization).

Conference Theme

How to enhance the effect of educating lean? This is the key question which will be addressed by a number of keynote speakers and presenters in parallel sessions, workshops and panel discussions. A careful choice of the content and the way of teaching is needed in order to maximize customer value and to minimize the learning waste.

About the European Lean Educator Conference

The European Lean Educator Conference provides a platform for lean managers, trainers, coaches, researchers and teachers from industry, higher education and service organizations to share their knowledge and experiences on educating lean. The practical know-how of all participants allows for lively discussions, sharing experience and expertise.
